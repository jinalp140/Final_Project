Kashmiri Pulao

Kashmiri cuisine (Kashmiri: कॉशुर खयॊन / kashmiri; Kashur khyon; Urdu: کشمیری پکوان‎) is the cuisine of the Kashmir Valley region. Rice is the staple food of Kashmiris and has been so since ancient times. Meat, along with rice, is the most popular food item in Kashmir. Kashmiris consume meat voraciously. Despite being Brahmin, some Kashmiri Pandits are meat eaters Main Ingrediant: Basmati Rice, Some vegetables and indian spices
